INTRODUCING

REGALIUM

Designed for India's most progressive communities, Regalium is a revolutionary mixed-use hub in Koramangala, Bengaluru. We elevate how our exclusive community can play, indulge, shop, connect, and work through personalized services, advanced technological capabilities, meticulous curation, and a bold new vision for the future.

THE LOCATION

Our location strategically represents both our innovative vision and the Machani Group legacy. As the heart of the dynamic city of Bengaluru, Koramangala is the perfect location for our exclusive group of members to come together. There is also a growing gap between people, physical spaces, and culture in Bengaluru, and by being situated at Koramangala, Regalium is uniquely poised to succeed as a luxurious and thriving community hub.

Privacy, Exclusivity, & Authenticity

Regalium's bold new vision for the future is highlighted in every part of the building. Each space is designed to enhance and empower its users with cutting-edge technological features and smart, thoughtful design interventions.

The Regal Boulevard

Regalium prioritises occupants' well-being while setting new standards for business ease. Its laws foster a vibrant economy and society. Guided by GRI Standards for ESG, it enhances health through materiality assessments. As a sustainable architecture model, it reflects Machani's dedication to ethical business and community engagement. Continuous GHG monitoring and waste management highlight its technology-driven sustainability. Stakeholder engagement aligns with occupants' needs, bolstering health, safety, and well-being. Rigorous risk management enhances Regalium's resilience and sustainability commitment.

The Regal Tableaus

The next wave of innovative OOH advertising displays for luxury brands, enabling storytelling in the most interactive and immersive way. Placed on the outer walls of the boulevard, the tableau can be used by brands to display their latest campaigns, advertisements. The multi purpose display can also be converted to extra screens during an event.

The Regal Gallery

The Regal Gallery invites visitors as they arrive at Regalium. This double-storied, naturally lit space is airy, grand, and dynamic—it is designed to host events, exhibitions, shows, and more. The Gallery is designed to ensure the highest level of audio-visual experience: it has flexible, soundproof walls, a discrete sound system built into the space, and Smart Glass for private, exclusive events. It is also enabled with innovative technology to facilitate immersive experiences. Art galleries can also access several futuristic features including a temperature-controlled vault and blockchain integration for artwork provenance. We envision the Regal Gallery to host shows that include immersive experiences, tangible NFTs, AR/holographic/VR theater, and historical site exploration.

The Regal Vaults

Enhancing the shopping experience at Regalium, the Regal Vaults are exclusive private lounges where members can try, choose, and experience products from different brands. The Vaults facilitate this immersive experience via personal shoppers, VR/AR technologies, smart mirrors, and AI assistants. The space itself can be customized per member preferences, and shoppers can browse and try products from the comfort of the Vault without needing to visit the physical store—making the experience hyper-personalized and seamless.

The Regal Gastronomy

Bringing together the best chefs and a curated mix of fine dining and casual dining experiences, this section houses some of the best global brands.
