REGALIUM

Designed for India's most progressive communities, Regalium is a revolutionary mixed-use hub in Koramangala, Bengaluru. We elevate how our exclusive community can play, indulge, shop, connect, and work through personalized services, advanced technological capabilities, meticulous curation, and a bold new vision for the future.

THE LOCATION

Our location strategically represents both our innovative vision and the Machani Group legacy. As the heart of the dynamic city of Bengaluru, Koramangala is the perfect location for our exclusive group of members to come together. There is also a growing gap between people, physical spaces, and culture in Bengaluru, and by being situated at Koramangala, Regalium is uniquely poised to succeed as a luxurious and thriving community hub.
