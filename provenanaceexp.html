THE SEVEN
TENETS ​OF
COMMUNITY​, COLLABORATIOn
​& AUTHENTICITY

Drawing upon evocative analogies, each floor at the Provenance transports occupants into realms of extraordinary experiences, elevating the notion of luxury to unparalleled heights.Each tenet, each floor, speaks to a unique target group, focused towards enhancing efficiency and effectiveness while at work.

The studio

The lobby is an energetic hub with artistic and digital exhibits for media industries; corridors showcase ongoing works, and decks provide a colorful, collaborative space for networking and brainstorming.

Potential clients on this floor could include media and PR firms, marketing and advertising agencies, creative collectives etc.

THE forum

The Forum features a refined lobby with a minimalist design and art symbolizing law, soundproofed corridors with legal updates, and decks with outdoor lounges for client meetings, all overlooking the cityscape.

Potential clients on this floor could include law firms, IB/MBB consulting firms.

THE LAB

The Lab showcases a futuristic lobby with interactive tech displays, corridors blending collaboration and privacy with tech-inspired elements, and decks featuring modular workspaces for tech demonstrations and interactive experiences.

Potential clients on this floor could include R&D centres, innovation labs, tech startups.

THE EXCHANGE

The Exchange boasts a modern, vibrant lobby with digital stock tickers and currency designs, corridors designed for spontaneous collaboration with data screens, and a sophisticated deck area for networking and workshops in fintech.

Potential clients on this floor could include fintech companies and firms, tech startups.

THE HIVE

The Hive features a lively, open lobby with communal tables and idea boards for co-working, corridors with flexible workspaces and inspirational decor, and versatile decks for workshops and casual collaboration in a community-focused setting.

Potential clients on this floor could include think tanks, incubators, innovation labs, co-working space.

THE LOFT

The Loft is an elite, members-only floor featuring a luxurious lobby with premium furnishings and art for distinguished professionals, quiet corridors with private alcoves for exclusivity, and elegant decks with fine dining and panoramic views for high-profile clientele.

This floor could act as an exclusive space where only a few discerning professionals/companies and private club members would have regular access.

THE TERRACE

The Terrace is a private club floor with a sumptuous lobby offering VIP services, corridors adorned with prestigious art leading to exclusive areas, and a members-only deck with gourmet dining, private cabanas, and entertainment, embodying luxury and relaxation.

This floor could act as a private club with members only area, direct access to fine-dining restaurants, clubs and programming.
