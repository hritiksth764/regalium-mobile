Provenance is a future forward, sacred space that ignites communities to do and be more. Here, within this boundless realm, a sanctuary blossoms - a haven of sustainability, where human essence and technological innovation intertwine and nurture collaboration that is curated for trailblazers and visionaries.

THE LOCATION

There are several reasons why Machani Group decided to choose Koramangala, Bengaluru as their innovation hub. One of them is to pay homage to its ancestors who were far ahead of their time, and built things that have lasted for generations. Machani's vision to create something in the heart of the city, stems from the need to address a growing gap between people, physical spaces, and culture. Strategically Koramangala is also known as the billionaire's street of India, making it the right T.A.G for Regalium.

The Regal Gallery

The Regal Gallery invites visitors as they arrive at Regalium. This double-storied, naturally lit space is airy, grand, and dynamic—it is designed to host events, exhibitions, shows, and more. The Gallery is designed to ensure the highest level of audio-visual experience: it has flexible, soundproof walls, a discrete sound system built into the space, and Smart Glass for private, exclusive events. It is also enabled with innovative technology to facilitate immersive experiences. Art galleries can also access several futuristic features including a temperature-controlled vault and blockchain integration for artwork provenance. We envision the Regal Gallery to host shows that include immersive experiences, tangible NFTs, AR/holographic/VR theater, and historical site exploration.

The Regal Boulevard

Running along Regalium's South facade on the ground level, the Regal Boulevard is a dynamic outdoor space and our flagship venue. With lush greenery and modular seating options, the Boulevard is where users can enjoy an extension of Regalium's luxury interiors as well as access evolving pop-ups and activities. The Regal Boulevard's luxurious plug-and-play capabilities—for hosting events like live performances, exhibitions, product launches, conferences, and film productions—is a welcome addition to the city of Bengaluru, where such venues are scarce.
