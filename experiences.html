DRIVEN WITH INNOVATION

JOURNEY INTO
THE FUTURE

Regalium is anchored in the four cornerstones of Luxury, Heritage, Innovation, Community. As a singular destination, Regalium fosters art, culture, and innovation. Our approach to luxury is founded on hyper-personalized, tech-driven services that anticipate our user's needs—establishing Regalium's values as a pioneering, futuristic risk-taker.

EXCLUSIVE ACCESS

With exclusive access for the luxury retail audience, Regalium ensures an intimate and bespoke shopping journey, free from the usual hustle and tailored to the discerning few.

EXCLUSIVE RETAIL

Regalium's retail spaces are designed around a stimulating walkthrough of some of the world's leading brands—in the style of luxury stores like Harrods and Selfridges. Regalium will also bring together established brands from around the world as well as emerging homegrown brands from India, shining a spotlight on quality, craftsmanship, and new notions of luxury. Regalium also offers shoppers customized, futuristic services to elevate their shopping experience and extend it beyond the four walls of Regalium.

THE WHISPERING
PASSAGES

The Whispering Passages are quiet corridors and gathering areas with high sound absorption capabilities that enable rest and private conversations, strategically placed between stores and sections. These passages enhance the overall shopping experience, providing customers with a quiet and serene environment for various activities during their shopping journey.

SPHERE BY SVASA LIFE

The Regalium app allows interested individuals to sign up for digital club membership, which can later extend to physical membership for access to exclusive areas. To maintain exclusivity, a verification process or referral program is mandatory. The app is integrated with the centralised payment system, enabling seamless purchases using linked credit cards or digital wallets.

PAYSTREAM BY
REGALIUM

Additionally, we look at providing a dedicated mobile app with features like product catalogs, personalized offers, and seamless payment options, and the NFC enabled phones will become your club access cards. Members can link their preferred payment methods through registration and shop at various outlets using their mobile app, and each purchase is instantly recorded in a centralized accounting system. The system tracks all purchases in real-time, providing a running total of their expenditure. When ready to leave, members can visit a payment counter or have a relationship manager assist with billing, and a consolidated bill reflecting all purchases is generated.

THE REGAL NAVIGATION

A dynamic Personalized Journey system that leads from parking onto the main floors and is integrated with the other member-only features of Regalium. AI-driven Wayfinding Kiosks enable curated journeys for visitors while Instant Curation offers digital assistance. The Personalized Journey is integrated with other features of Regalium, such as The Regal Stewards and The Regal Portal, ensuring a seamless experience from arrival to departure.
